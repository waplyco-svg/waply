About Waply

Built for spaces where the Wi-Fi needs to actually work.

Waply focuses on Wi-Fi heat maps, wireless site surveys, and access point placement guidance for homes, offices, and other spaces where coverage quality affects how people actually work and connect.

Some jobs need predictive planning before equipment is moved. Others need live validation to confirm dead zones, overlap, or underperforming access point placement. Waply is built around that distinction instead of forcing every job into the same generic solution.

Estimates are scoped to the space itself, the layout complexity, and whether the work needs to be remote, on-site, or both.

Who It’s For

Residential and commercial work.
